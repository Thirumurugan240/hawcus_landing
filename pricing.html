Common questions

Is Rs 4,999 really everything?

Yes. Every feature in the table is in the plan. We do not hold features back for a higher tier or charge extra for the dialer, the AI features, the integrations or the reports.

What if I need more than 3 users?

The plan covers three users as standard. Additional users are added at a flat rate, and we will quote it up front rather than making you find it in a pricing table.

Are WhatsApp message charges included?

WhatsApp bills conversation charges directly through Meta at their published rates. Those are passed through at cost, with nothing added on top by us.

What happens to my data if I leave?

You can export your leads, conversations and pipeline at any time. It is your data, and there is no exit fee or lock-in period.
